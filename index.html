Tranquil Travel
Making travel peaceful

Dear Traveller,
Tranquil Travel is a site dedicated to help you plan your tours. Using JavaScript, CSS and HTML, I created a check list. You can use this to keep track of the items you have to take. You can add any item you need, delete items when no longer necessary and also cross out an item once you're done taking it.
A Rest API was utilised to enable you to check the weather at your destination, so that you can pack accordingly.
An HTML form has also been provided, which redirects you to TripAdvisor’s search page based on your queries, to book hotels and discover things to do at your travel destination.
